Delicious Grilled Cheese!

Description

A deliciously simple and quick recipe

Who would have thought that you'd be reading a recipe on how to make grilled cheese? Well here you are! Whether in a rush, or just lounging on the couch, while binging on Netflix, this recipe will work for you!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 slices of American Cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • Kosher salt

Process

  1. Start by heating up 1/2 of the butter in a large heavy bottom pan over medium heat

  2. Place the 2 slices of bread in the pan and cook until the bottoms are golden brown.

  3. Carefully flip the bread over and place the two slices of cheese on the bread. Add half of the remaining butter and cook until the breaad is golden brown.

  4. Once again, carefully flip the now sandwhich over and add the reaming half of butter to the pan. Cook until golden brown.

  5. Carefully remove the sandwhich from the pan and give it a sprinkle of kosher salt
