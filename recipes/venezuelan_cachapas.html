Venezuelan Cachapas

Description

A staple Venezuelan food that will immediatly transport you to the Carribean part of South America. It is best paired with mozarella cheese, but you can also enjoy it with a grille Italian Sausage

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of cooked corn
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 3 tablesppons of Harina P.A.N.
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespon of sugar
  • salt
  • 2 tablespoon of butter
  • Mozarella cheese and Italian Sausage(optional)

Process

  1. Start blending the corn, milk, Harina P.A.N., sugar, salt, and 1/2 of butter.

  2. Once it is blended, add the two eggs and give it a few pulses

  3. Warm up a cast iron pan and add the remaining half of butter. Carefully pour out the blended batter, and shape into a circle.

  4. Cook the batter until it is golden brown and the cachapa feels firm. Flip it over and cook until golden brown once again.

  5. Remove from pan, place on plate, and fold in half. If you like, you can fill the cachapa with mozarella cheese, sausage, or both.
